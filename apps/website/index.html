❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FoundersTribe.

Is FoundersTribe free to use? ▾
Yes! FoundersTribe is free to download and use. Core features like browsing and connecting with founders are free. Premium features like unlimited matching, AI tools, and advanced filters are available through our affordable subscription plans.
How does the founder matching algorithm work? ▾
Our AI-powered algorithm analyzes your profile — skills, industry, startup stage, location preferences, and personality traits — and cross-references them with other founders to surface the most compatible matches. It continuously learns from your interactions to improve recommendations over time.
How are profiles verified? ▾
All users are required to sign up with a valid email address. Founders can optionally link their LinkedIn, GitHub, or portfolio to add credibility. Our moderation team reviews profiles and removes spam or fake accounts to maintain a high-quality community.
Can I use FoundersTribe to find freelancers too? ▾
Absolutely! FoundersTribe has a built-in freelancer marketplace where you can post gigs, review proposals, and hire vetted talent across design, development, marketing, and more — all within the same app.
Is my data safe with FoundersTribe? ▾
Yes. We take data privacy very seriously. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal information to third parties. You can request account deletion at any time. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.
Which platforms is FoundersTribe available on? ▾
FoundersTribe is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). We are also working on a web version for even broader accessibility.
