## Design Context

### Users
Ambitious early-stage founders seeking co-founders and freelancers. They're time-pressed, high-energy, and used to premium product experiences (Superhuman, Linear, Notion). They open the landing page to evaluate whether this tool is serious enough for their startup journey. Context: browsing on laptops during work hours or phones late at night while researching.

### Brand Personality
**Bold, fast, sharp** — like a pitch deck that closes deals. Confident, direct, high-energy. Not playful, not corporate. The intersection of YC credibility and Superhuman craft.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: High-contrast dark theme. Editorial confidence. Dense information presented cleanly. Not "startup bro" — more "the tool serious founders actually use."
- **References**: YC (credibility, no-nonsense copy), Superhuman (premium product craft, speed), Linear (dark UI, sharp type, intentional motion)
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS landing pages, gradient-heavy AI startup sites, LinkedIn's corporate blandness
- **Theme**: Dark — founders browse late, product is dark-first, matches app aesthetic

### Design Principles
1. **Speed over spectacle** — Every element should feel fast and purposeful. No decorative fluff.
2. **Earned confidence** — Bold claims backed by specifics. No vague promises.
3. **Sharp hierarchy** — Clear visual order. The eye knows exactly where to go.
4. **Density with clarity** — Pack information tightly but never let it feel cramped.
5. **One red thread** — Brand red (#CF2030) used surgically, never sprayed everywhere.
